About Our School

Overview

Aputuogya D/A Junior High School is a respected basic education institution dedicated to shaping young minds through quality education, discipline, and moral upbringing. The school has served the community for over 10+ years.

History

Established to improve access to education in the community, the school has grown steadily and produced many successful students who continue their education in senior high schools across Ghana.

Mission

To provide a safe and supportive learning environment that builds knowledge, discipline, creativity, and responsibility in students.

Vision

To become a leading junior high school known for academic excellence, innovation, and producing future leaders.

Core Values

  • Discipline
  • Respect
  • Integrity
  • Hard Work
  • Excellence
  • Leadership

Academics

Subjects include Mathematics, English, Science, ICT, Social Studies, RME, and Creative Arts, following the Ghana Education Service curriculum.

Teaching

We use student-centered learning, combining modern ICT tools and traditional methods to improve understanding and engagement.

Facilities

The school provides classrooms, learning resources, ICT access, and space for sports and extracurricular activities.

Student Life

Students participate in sports, cultural events, clubs, and leadership activities that build confidence and teamwork.

Staff

Dedicated teachers and leadership staff ensure discipline, academic excellence, and student success.

Community

The school plays a major role in community development by educating future leaders and responsible citizens.

Achievement

Strong BECE performance with many students gaining admission into top senior high schools.
